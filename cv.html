Projects
Protein-based Porous Materials Templated from Pickering High Internal Phase Emulsions
$ The natural-based porous materials have become a popular development tendency. However, low
fracture toughness or mechanical strength is still a big drawback of these materials.So our work opens
a simple and feasible way to produce these porous materials.
$ Using gliadin-chitosan-complex-particles(GCCP) fabricated high internal phase emulsions(HIPEs),
then porous materials were obtained through solvent evaporation.
$ We were used Gelatin solution as aqueous phase to prepare GCCP stabilized HIPEs.After gelatin
was cooled, the freeze-dried porous materials with strong mechanical strength were fabricated by
salt-treating with the help of Hofmeister eﬀect.
$ These nontoxic and biodegradable porous materials, especially with favorable mechanical strength,
have potential application in many ﬁelds involving biomedical and material science, such as cell cul-
ture, (bio)catalysis, and wound or bone defect healing.
