Projects
Protein-based Porous Materials Templated from Pickering High Internal Phase Emulsions
$ Using gliadin-chitosan-complex-particles(GCCP) fabricated high internal phase
emulsions(HIPEs), then porous materials were obtained through solvent evapora-
tion.
$ We were used Gelatin solution as aqueous phase to prepare GCCP stabilized
HIPEs.After gelatin was cooled, the freeze-dried porous materials with strong me-
chanical strength were fabricated by salt-treating with the help of Hofmeister eﬀect.
$ These nontoxic and biodegradable porous materials, especially with favorable me-
chanical strength, have potential application in many ﬁelds involving biomedical
and material science, such as cell culture, (bio)catalysis, and wound or bone defect
healing.
